Privacy Policy

Your conversations stay on your iPhone. We never see them. Nobody does.

Talking Jellyfish does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All AI processing runs entirely on your device.

Zero data. Zero tracking.

How the App Works

All AI processing — including the language model, text-to-speech, and speech recognition — runs locally on your iPhone's processor. There is no cloud server. There is no analytics service. Your conversations never leave your device.

Internet Connections

The app works without any internet connection. The only network activity occurs when you choose to download optional AI models. These downloads retrieve model files only — no personal data is sent.

Voice and Audio

When you use voice mode, audio is processed in real time by on-device speech recognition. Audio is never stored, recorded, or transmitted. When you clone a voice, the audio file is processed locally and the resulting voice data stays on your device.

In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's own privacy policy governs the purchase transaction.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Since the app collects no data whatsoever, there is no data to protect — because there is no data at all.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page. Given our commitment to on-device processing, we do not anticipate changes that would involve data collection.
