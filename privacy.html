Privacy Policy

Talking Jellyfish: AI Friends

Talking Jellyfish does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything runs entirely on your device.

The Short Version

We built Talking Jellyfish to be completely private. There is no cloud server. There is no analytics service. There is no tracking. Your conversations, your voice recordings, and your data stay on your iPhone and nowhere else.

Data We Collect

None. We collect zero data from you. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not collect usage analytics
  • We do not collect crash reports
  • We do not use advertising identifiers
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels
  • We do not share data with third parties

How the App Works

All AI processing — including the language model, text-to-speech, and speech recognition — runs locally on your iPhone's processor. Your conversations never leave your device.

Internet Connections

The app requires no internet connection for normal use. The only network activity occurs when you choose to download optional AI models. These downloads connect to huggingface.co to retrieve model files. No personal data is sent during these downloads.

Voice Recordings

When you use voice mode, audio is processed in real time by on-device speech recognition. Audio is not stored, recorded, or transmitted. When you clone a voice, the audio file you provide is processed locally and the resulting voice data stays on your device.

In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's own privacy policy governs the purchase transaction.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Since the app collects no data whatsoever, there is no children's data to protect — because there is no data at all.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page. Given our commitment to on-device processing, we do not anticipate changes that would involve data collection.
